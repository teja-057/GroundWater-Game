About the Game

Welcome to our educational game designed to teach players about groundwater conservation and management. Our goal is to engage users in an interactive and fun way, while raising awareness about the importance of managing underground water resources.

  • Educational: Learn about groundwater management through videos, articles and decision-making scenarios.
  • Interactive: Make decisions that impact the virtual world and see the results of your choices.
  • Progress Tracking: Earn points, compete on the leaderboard, and get recognized for your water-smart choices.
  • Accessible:Register to save your progress and get certified.
